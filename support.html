Help & Support

You can ask a psychologist by going "Ask a psychologist" service and then you can read frequently faced issues over there and submit your questions anonymously .
How to find my mindmate?
Finding a mindmate involves seeking connections with people who share similar interests and values. In our "Personal Listener" service we hear your preference and assign your mindmate accordingly
How to take an appointment?
To take an appointment, go to "Contact" and provide necessary information we will reach you out with an appointment
How to take the psychological tests?
There are two psychological tests under our "Psychological Tests" service. User can select one and takes quizes to guess their psychological condition.
What can I learn from the wellness journal?
You will able to learn about various topics like Anxiety, Trauma from our "Wellness Journal" Service.
Where can I find 24/7 support?
Our "Mindmate" service provides 24/7 online help.
